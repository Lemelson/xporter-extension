How XPorter works

Everything runs inside your browser. There are no XPorter servers, no account, and no analytics or tracking. Your data goes straight from X into the file you download — it never passes through us.

XPorter reads the same public data you already see when you're logged in, using the X session that's already in your browser. It never asks for your password and never stores your login.

Posts, followers, following, and verified followers. Post exports include the full engagement metrics — views, likes, reposts, replies, quotes, and bookmarks — plus dates, links, media, and hashtags.

Pick the format that fits your workflow: CSV for any spreadsheet, XLSX for Excel, or JSON for developers. Files are built locally and download straight to your device.

Export posts from a specific time window. Date-range exports open an X search tab and scroll it automatically to gather matching posts — just keep that tab open until it finishes.

Progress is saved as the export runs, so you can pause, close the popup, or even restart your browser and continue where you left off. XPorter also paces its requests to respect X's limits and avoid temporary blocks.
